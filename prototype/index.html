How does this work?

If you're looking to purchase a new scaley friend, head on over to the Marketplace! You'll be able to search for the specific species, morph, sex, age, and/or vendor!

Why should I purchase a reptile online?

When you purchase from our Marketplace, you are exposed to a significantly wider variety of reptiles than you would at your typical pet store. Vendors ship overnight so that your new friend will be as stress-free as possible!

What if I want to become a vendor?

That's easy! When you go to make your account, you will select whether you are wanting to create a customer account or a vendor account. To become a vendor, you'll need to complete various security checks and set up a payment method.
